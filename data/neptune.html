Like Uranus, Neptune gets its bright blue color from methane gas in its atmosphere.
Storms not unlike Jupiter's Great Red Spot continuously travel across Neptune's surface.

Diameter – 30,776 miles
Distance from Sun – 2,795,084,800 miles
Length of Year – 60,190 days
Length of Day – 16 hours, 7 minutes
Average Temp - -353F
Gravity – approx. 1.1 times the gravity of Earth

Great Dark Spot – believed to be a hole in the atmosphere.
In 1994, Hubble Space Telescope revealed that the spot had disappeared.
